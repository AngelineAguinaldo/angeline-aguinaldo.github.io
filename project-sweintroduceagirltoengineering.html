Society of Women Engineers (SWE): Introduce a Girl to Engineering Day Video

This video showcases the integration of STEAM (Science, Technology, Engineering, Art, and Mathematics) integration in everyday life. The intended audience is young students in grades K through 12. This video is broken up into three parts:

  1. Definition of STEAM and examples of its presence in everyday life
  2. Projects being conducted at Drexel University's ExCITe Center that integrate art in their highly innovative research projects.
  3. How STEAM concepts can find their way into the classroom and at home

This video was showcased at the keynote address during Drexel University's Introduce a Girl to Engineering Day hosted by Drexel University's Society of Women Engineers.

Project Details

This was a small-scale film project sponsored by Drexel's Society of Women Engineers (SWE).
