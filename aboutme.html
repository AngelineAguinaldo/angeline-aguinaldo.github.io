About Me

Education

I am an undergraduate student at Drexel University in Philadelphia, PA. I will be graduating from an Accelerated Master of Science in Electrical Engineering/Bachelor of Science in Biomedical Engineering 5-Year Degree Program in June 2017. I will also be graduating with a graduate-level certificate in Engineering Management.

I attended high school at the Academy of Allied Health Sciences in Scotch Plains, New Jersey.

I am interested in pursuing a Ph.D. in the area of automated image analysis and computer vision.

Hobbies and Interests

I like to dabble in a lot of random interests of which include, but are not limited to: photography, ukelele, piano, fitness training, film-making, basketball, online thrift selling, musical theater, theological history, video-editing, singing, homelessness advocacy programs, learning computer programming languages, designing image analysis algorithms, making PowerPoint presentations, watching YouTube videos, organizing and formating documents, improving computer folder structures, etc.

However, it can be probably be said that there are few unifying aspects that bridge these pastimes together:

  • Critical thinking. Because why not? Everyone loves a challenge.
  • Creative thinking. I am all for freedom of expression which makes creative thinking a must!
  • Visualization. Because for some reason, the things I'm really-super-good at involve images, diagrams, and/or making things "look good".
  • Self-improvement. A natural by-product of trying new things (which is probably why my list is so extensive and all over the place).
